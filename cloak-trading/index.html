Cloak Your Trading And Minimize Frontrunning

Have you ever noticed when you are trading a coin, the price goes up just before you are about to buy or right before you finish your order? Professionals call this frontrunning, there are already market makers and trading bots that predict your trading behavior and trade ahead of you to make money.

CoinFi is porting the same trading algorithms hedge fund traders use such as VWAP, Sniper, Iceberg, Implementation Shortfall and Inline to cloak your trading behavior to give you an trade execution advantage. Screenshot on the right displays how this cloaking behavior works.
