What is CoinFi?

CoinFi provides timely, curated cryptocurrency financial intelligence.

We believe that the mainstream adoption of blockchain technology hinges on economic incentives. Or to stop beating around the bush: the more people make money from cryptocurrency, the more it will help drive usage across the world.

The best way to make money in this space is through the thorough understanding of both the underlying technology as well as the market dynamics.

CoinFi researches, analyzes, and curates cryptocurrency news and thought pieces to expose you to the cream of the crop content that you need to stay up-to-date in this rapidly changing industry.
